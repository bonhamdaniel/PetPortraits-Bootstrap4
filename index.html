About Kathryn Bonham

Kathryn Bonham is a Canadian pet portrait artist specializing in traditionally painted portraits of both dogs and horses. Educated at Sheridan College, Kathryn mainly focuses on oil portraiture and strives to create realistic images that truly capture the subject's look and personality.

Kathryn is currently working out of studio space in Burlington, ON and is accepting commissions from clients around the globe. Pricing starting at $250.

Please note that a high quality reference image in desired pose is required in order to produce a detailed portrait, cellphone images will not suffice.
